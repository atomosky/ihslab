Intelligent Health Solutions Lab

Led by Dr. Huanmei Wu · Department of Health Services Administration & Policy · Temple University Barnett College of Public Health

The Intelligent Health Solutions (IHS) Lab applies artificial intelligence, data science, and knowledge graph technologies to improve population health and advance health equity. We collaborate across academic, clinical, industry, governmental, and community settings to create data-driven solutions that are transparent, ethical, and equitable.
